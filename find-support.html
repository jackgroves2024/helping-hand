Find Support for Your Mental Health Journey

Below you'll find a variety of resources to help you access mental health support in the UK, whether you're looking for therapy, support groups, or urgent assistance. Take the next step in your mental wellness journey.

Therapy Services

Therapy can be a vital part of maintaining good mental health. It provides a safe, confidential space to talk through your feelings, understand your mental health issues, and work on strategies for coping and improving your emotional well-being.

How It Helps: Therapy helps you understand the root causes of your stress or mental health struggles, guide you through difficult emotions, and provide strategies to help you manage your day-to-day life.

Find a Therapist

Support Groups

Support groups bring together individuals who share similar challenges, offering a unique chance to connect with others and share experiences in a safe, non-judgmental environment.

How It Helps: Support groups provide a sense of community. Sometimes, knowing you're not alone can make all the difference in improving mental health. Members can share coping strategies, offer advice, and provide reassurance.

Find a Group

Crisis Helplines

Crisis helplines provide confidential, non-judgmental, and immediate help for those in need of urgent mental health support. These services are available 24/7 and are a vital resource for people experiencing a mental health crisis.

How It Helps: Crisis helplines provide a lifeline in moments of immediate distress. They help de-escalate potentially dangerous situations and offer practical advice on next steps.

Call Samaritans

Online Mental Health Resources

Online mental health resources provide easy access to a wealth of information, tools, and guidance on mental health topics. These resources can help individuals manage their mental health at their own pace.

How It Helps: Online resources are a convenient way to access information and support at any time. They provide an excellent starting point for anyone looking to learn more about their mental health or looking for ways to manage their emotions or behavior.

Explore Resources

Employee Assistance Programs (EAPs)

EAPs provide free, confidential support for employees experiencing personal or work-related challenges, including mental health issues. Many UK employers offer EAPs as part of their employee benefits.

How It Helps: EAPs provide professional support for issues affecting mental health, including workplace stress, anxiety, or personal issues.

Find EAP Resources

NHS Support

The NHS provides a range of mental health services across the UK, including talking therapies, community mental health teams, and emergency mental health care.

How It Helps: NHS services offer comprehensive care for all ages, and provide access to counseling, therapy, and medication management.

Visit NHS Services
